Here's a simple recipe for homemade ramen:

Ingredients:
- 2 packs of ramen noodles
- 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
- 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
- Sliced green onions
- Soft-boiled egg
- Sliced carrots
- Sliced mushrooms
- Cooked chicken, pork, or tofu (optional)

Instructions:
1. In a pot, bring the broth to a boil and add the soy sauce and sesame oil.
2. Add the ramen noodles and cook according to the package instructions.
3. In the meantime, prepare your toppings by saut¨¦ing the vegetables and protein in a separate pan.
4. Once the noodles are cooked, assemble your ramen bowl by placing the noodles at the bottom and adding the broth on top.
5. Top with your cooked vegetables, protein, green onions, and a soft-boiled egg.
6. Enjoy your homemade ramen hot!

Feel free to customize your ramen with your favorite toppings and seasonings. Enjoy your delicious homemade ramen!
